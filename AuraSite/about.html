Who We Are

Aura Group is focused on technology and business services. The company is an associate of the CIE Group of Companies that has been operating in Zimbabwe since 1980. As organizations look to improve their performance, they want service providers that follow proven approaches to solve immediate business challenges and help define and deliver next-generation innovations. Aura Group’s industrialized global delivery capabilities provide clients with the right people, skills and industry expertise to deliver management consulting, technology and business process outsourcing services with greater reliability, quality and productivity.

What we stand for

The success of our business is due to our detailed knowledge and experience of the African market. Over the years, we have developed a keen awareness of how to accomplish the business aims of our international and local clients. A team of committed employees embodies our sense of purpose and loyalty to our contacts.

Fearless in our approach and steadfast in our determination, we are well positioned to defend the interests of those companies which place their faith in our abilities. Due to the increasingly complex business and political climate prevailing within the African region, we have nurtured our operations by carefully using our resources and maintaining a high degree of flexibility.
