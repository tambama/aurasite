Professional Service Delivery

Business Development

We help companies acquire, develop and retain more profitable customer relationships through our broad range of capabilities that address every aspect of the customer experience. We help our partners accelerate growth, improve sales productivity and reduce customer-care costs to increase the value

of customer relationships and enhance the economic value of the brands and products of our partners. To date, we have represented global companies such as Microsoft, Dell, HP and Fujitsu in local African markets. We add value by providing a hands on approach to the markets whilst our principles focus on strategic issues and product development.

Business Consulting

We provide independent advice on complex business situations at senior levels in the public and private sectors. Our team takes a hands-on role to ensure solutions are not only conceived, but also delivered with genuine benefits. Aura Services supports its clients by cutting through complexity to deliver results. We plan, design and deliver solutions that result in real value through releasing potential efficiency gains from the implementation of organisational and ICT enabled business change. Our business consultancy focuses on what your requirements are, and tailor our services accordingly.

Through sharing best practices, providing mentoring, training and support, we will help your business reach its full potential. We take the time to understand your needs carefully analysing where you are right now and where you want to be in the future. The consultancy services offered include business, sales, marketing and brand strategies.

Project Management

Aura provides a service designed to help you complete your projects on time and within budget. We assist you with rollout of large scale projects, allowing you to focus on your core business objectives. Our industry experienced project managers are trained to guide project coordination, ommunication and project budget management to assure your projects are completed on schedule and within budget, avoiding costly overruns saving you both time and money. In addition to the above, we have also established vast business networks across the SADC region that spurs collaboration and co-operation on large scale national projects. All solutions and services are tailored to meet the specific needs of each customer.
